About Us



Our mission is to increase voter engagement by helping voters find their polling place as quickly as possible, with the click of a button.


According to the US Census Bureau, 35.7 million Americans move every single year! Whether it be moving to a new school, or finding a new residence, many Americans have moved since the last time they voted and participated in an election. Powered by Google's Civic API, we hope to drive more Americans to the polls by helping them quickly obtain the crucial information they need to make their voice heard in the upcoming election. We hope you enjoy using our site, and that you share FindMyVote.org with others so that they too can participate in the 2016 general election.
